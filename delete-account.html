🗑️ Delete Your Account

App: Money Merge: Tile Puzzle Game
Developer: ApexX Studio

What data we store

When you use Google Sign-in (optional), we store the following data linked to your account:

  • Firebase User ID (anonymous identifier)
  • Game progress (score, best tile, grid state)
  • Gem balance

We do not store your name, email address, or any payment information on our servers.

How to request account deletion

Step 1

Send an email to: support.apexxstudio@gmail.com

Step 2

Use subject line: Account Deletion Request — Money Merge

Step 3

Include your registered Google account email so we can locate and delete your data.

What gets deleted

  • ✅ Your Firebase account and associated UID
  • ✅ All Firestore game data linked to your account
  • ✅ Local device data is automatically cleared when you uninstall the app

Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 7 days.

In-app purchases

Purchase records are maintained by Google Play and RevenueCat for legal and financial compliance. These are not deleted as part of account deletion, in accordance with applicable law.
